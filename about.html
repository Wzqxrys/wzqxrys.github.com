About me

WZQXRYS - A Representation of Space

Most of my projects just like WZQXRYS started on the whiteboard,
A Cloaked Figure and I were in a conversation about coordinates, trigonometry, matrices and quaternions when they asked

“What would all those math concepts look like in a single picture?”

A storm of thoughts and a kalidascope of sketches form the beginnings of Wzqxrys

Each piece represents several things related to math and computer science
while primarily representing my brand of fine art I have and will create.
